What is VA Disability Claims Intelligence?

VA disability claims intelligence combines AI-powered legal research with real BVA decision data to help veterans understand their rating criteria, find grant patterns, and build stronger claims. Veteran2Veteran provides free access to tools that search thousands of Board of Veterans' Appeals decisions, navigate 38 CFR regulations, and analyze CAVC precedent -- resources that were once only available to attorneys and accredited claims agents.
